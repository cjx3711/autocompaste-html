Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

For this experiment, we will be testing the efficiency of a new copy pasting technique called AutoCompaste. You will be presented with a series of tests to complete.

What to look out for

When you are presented with a test, there are two main things to look out for. The technique you should be using, and the text you have to copy paste. These two details are highlighted in red for your convenience.

The two methods we will be testing are Autocompaste and the traditional copy paste method using Ctrl + C and Ctrl + V.

You will need to find the text in the windows, then copy and paste it into the textbox provided. The text is highlighted to assist you in the finding process.

Traditional method

Please use the keyboard shortcuts Ctrl + C and Ctrl + V.
Do not use your mouse to right click, copy, and right click paste.

Using autocompaste

Instead of highlighting the text, just start typing and it will provide an autocomplete dropdown. You can use the arrow keys to select what you want and then hit enter to select it. After doing that, you can press the right arrow key to select more text to be pasted.
