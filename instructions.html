Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

For this experiment, we will be testing the efficiency of a new copy pasting technique called AutoCompaste. You will be presented with a series of tests to complete.

What to look out for

When you are presented with a test, there are two main things to look out for.

These two details are highlighted in red for your convenience.

  1. The technique you should be using.
    • TRADITONAL
    • AUTOCOMPASTE
  2. The text you have to copy paste.

What to do

  1. Find text in the many windows.
  2. Copy paste the text from the window into the Text Editor
  3. Text is highlighted for convenience.

Traditional method

Please use the keyboard shortcuts Ctrl + C and Ctrl + V.
Do not use your mouse to right click, copy, and right click paste.

Using autocompaste

  1. Just start typing in text box
    • An autocomplete dropdown will appear
  2. Up/Down to select the option
  3. Enter to paste.
  4. Left/Right to paste more.
